INTERNSHIP FAIR_17

DATE:8th April 2017
VENUE:NIEC,AUDI
ABOUT:
The ECell of Northern India Engineering College organized the Internship fair 2017 to bring a wide range of opportunity under one roof. On 8th April 2017 more than 50 companies with more than five hundred internship offers. The Fair was a platform where students could seek a mentor in top companies get trained to face the market and work as an intern. The fair wasn’t limited to just profiles of technical interest like robotics, product engineering but all possible field of content development, marketing, social entrepreneurship, data analytics and much more.With Gradlimited E-Cell NIEC gave them a free chance to learn to write a perfect resume under professional guidance. Top leading companies like IBM and LIC came forward to hire students from various backgrounds. Startups like CoHo, Nuts and Bolts, encipher and many more brands provided the budding entrepreneurs the opportunity to face the market under their guidance. With amazing media partners The Education Tree and DU updates and E-Cell NIEC, the footfall for the fair was more than 1000 students from all across Delhi NCR. With so many companies on board, more than 400 students were able to get the internship post the screening. With such a big event and such great success, the E-Cell NIEC continues to nurture the motto “ Think Entrepreneur, bleed Entrepreneur”.

SUMMIT_17

DATE:31st January 2017
VENUE:NIEC,AUDI
ABOUT:
 To encourage the spirit of Entrepreneurship and foster young minds The ECell of Northern India Engineering College organized its first big event for 2017 E-summit’17. On 31st January 2017, we had India's top 5 leading industrial giants on the same platform to share their experiences and bridging the gap between budding ideas and their implementations. The national level speakers included Mr. Onkar K Khullar popularly known as "Digital Gandhi". A name of the multi talented successful ideal in the world of Entrepreneurship. A Social Entrepreneur, TedX Speaker, Blogger, Business Consultant, Philosopher and what not. 1 person living 10 lives with the motto to leave an impact on the world. Married to coding and affair with Entrepreneurship Mr. Ankush Singla, Proud Co-Founder of Coding Blocks and Coding Ninjas. Started by IIT Delhi then Stanford University he took his skill to the next level working with big companies like Facebook and Amazon. Singla was a living legend in the programming world. The Summit was greatly honored by one of the leading ladies in the business world founder of Ploysz Multiservices Pvt. Ltd and Carbon fixers Payal Rastogi This lady is a living example of woman power and the "never give up" spirit of an entrepreneur. Mr. Rahul Tyagi, Vice President at Lucideus, The life of this man can be summed up within the 5 genres An Entrepreneurs, Cyber Space Researcher, Ethical Hacker, Trainer and An Author. Born in an ordinary family of Punjab, he shared his success story and his desire to go an extra mile. The last speaker of the day was the proud alumni of NIEC Mr. Rahul Juneja CEO of Let's Play. Being a fitness model himself he decided to revolutionize fitness scenario of the country and founded RJ let's play. This proved out to be India’s most sought-after fitness equipment servicing, maintenance, relocation and installation service provider E-Cell NIEC made the summit an interactive session with fun activities like Couch Potato, Startup War, Discovering Entrepreneur and Bizz quiz to nurture the moto of E-cell NIEC “ Think Entrepreneur, bleed Entrepreneur”

INTERNSHIP FAIR_16

DATE:10th October 2016
VENUE:NIEC,BLOCK-4
ABOUT:
 The Entrepreneurship Cell of NIEC hosted a one day Internship Fair. The fair brought on board various startups seeking interns in various fields, Employers from business, government and non-profit agencies hired people for full-time and part-time internships.
